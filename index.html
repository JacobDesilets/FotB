A 501C3 organization dedicated to raising awareness and support our local municipal golf course, Beaver Meadow, through charitable endeavors.

The Power of Building Community

At a municipal golf course, the game goes beyond fairways and greens—it’s about community. It’s where players of all backgrounds come together, friendships are formed over shared rounds, and local pride runs deep. This is where beginners learn, families connect, community development and lifelong memories are made. The true power of a municipal course lies in its ability to bring people together, making golf accessible to all, welcoming, and rooted in the heart of the community.
