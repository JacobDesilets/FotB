Our Mission

Our mission is to promote and preserve the accessibility of golf by advocating for the value and benefits of municipal golf courses.

We are dedicated to cultivating community engagement, fostering inclusivity, and enhancing the well being of individuals through the preservation of municipal golf courses.

Our mission is to champion the importance of municipal golf courses in providing affordable and equitable access to the sport of golf and other activities, while fostering environmental stewardship and the economic vitality of our communities.

We strive to create a lasting impact by promoting the social, recreational, and educational benefits of municipal golf courses, ensuring that these valuable green spaces remain accessible for generations to come.

We are committed to building strong partnerships, fostering public awareness, and generating support to maintain and enhance municipal golf courses, providing opportunities for individuals of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds to enjoy the game of golf and other outdoor activities.
